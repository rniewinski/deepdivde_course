# looks innocent. fires 1 + N queries. authors = Author.objects.all() for a in authors: print(a.name, a.books.count())
queries issued1 + 1,204
total time2.41 s
# the classic N+1. you've shipped this.
-- one query. the planner does the work. SELECT a.name, count(b.id) FROM authors a LEFT JOIN books b ON b.author_id = a.id GROUP BY a.name;
queries issued1
total time11 ms
-- ~219× faster. same data.
-- and here's how you'd prove it HashAggregate (actual rows=1204) -> Hash Left Join (rows=88k) Hash Cond: (b.author_id = a.id) -> Index Scan on books_author_idx
